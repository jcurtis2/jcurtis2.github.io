PartMC

Particle-resolved Monte Carlo code for atmospheric aerosol simulation

Authors:

  • Nicole Riemer
  • Matthew West

If you use PartMC, we request that you reference some of the papers on which PartMC is based:

  • R. E. L. DeVille, N. Riemer, and M. West, Weighted Flow Algorithms (WFA) for stochastic particle coagulation, Journal of Computational Physics 230(23), 8427-8451, doi:10.1016/j.jcp.2011.07.027, 2011.
  • N. Riemer, M. West, R. A. Zaveri, and R. C. Easter, Simulating the evolution of soot mixing state with a particle-resolved aerosol model, Journal of Geophysical Research 114(D09202), doi:10.1029/2008JD011073, 2009.
